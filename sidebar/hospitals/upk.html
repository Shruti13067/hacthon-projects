15 Years Experience
Shubham Hospital, in a very short span of time, has grown to be one of the top most hospitals in Varanasi. Aiming at delivering highest patient care facilities, the hospital houses a team of highly skilled professionals who are supported with latest generation diagnostic and treatment tools. Besides, the hospital has a splendid administrative team that always focuses at fastest response systems to patients and their families.

Salient Features

  1. A multispeciality hospital accommodating 150 beds
  2. Has been regarded as one of the best centers for surgical treatment of cardiac problems in and around Varanasi
  3. Offers a well designed blood bank along with 600 blood bank units
  4. 24/7 ambulance and emergency services
  5. Provides comprehensive diagnostic facilities with utmost accuracy
  6. One of the rare hospitals in region to have a unique IVF clinic
