Popular Hospital
Kakarmatta,Varanasi

30 Years Experience
Initiated and governed by Popular Medicare Limited, the hospital was started to provide general surgery and gynaecological services. However, soon it started widening its spheres to a number of medical faculties, and today it is one of the well known multispeciality centers in Varanasi. Housing an efficient team of doctors, supported by latest technical advents in medical domain, Popular Hospital is one name for effective and affordable health care.

Salient Features

  1. A multispecialty hospital accommodating 200 beds.
  2. All operating theaters meet international standards.
  3. Manages both IPDs and OPDs with utmost efficiencies and thus is one of the best in domain.
  4. Focuses on continuous upgradation of pharmacy that is available for 24 hours.
  5. Running 24 hour emergency units fitted with all required and advanced equipment.
  6. Laboratory with advanced apparatus and wide range of services.
